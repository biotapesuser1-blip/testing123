Unicycles are single-wheeled vehicles that require balance and coordination to ride effectively. This section introduces topics related to unicycle riding, maintenance, and fun activities.

## Introduction to Unicycles
Unicycles are unique and versatile vehicles known for their simplicity. They are commonly used in performance arts, outdoor activities, and even competitive sports.

## Riding Techniques
Learn how to master the art of unicycle riding, from basic balance exercises to advanced tricks.

## Maintenance
Proper maintenance ensures your unicycle remains safe and functional. Topics include:
- Tire care
- Pedal adjustments
- Frame inspection

## Activities and Community
Unicycling can be a social activity! Check out local clubs, competitions, and events to connect with other enthusiasts.
