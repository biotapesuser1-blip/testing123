---
title: Chain Care
sidebar: { order: 3 }
---

A clean and lubricated chain ensures smooth gear shifting and prevents rust. Regularly inspect your chain for signs of wear or damage, and replace it as needed.

To maintain your chain, use a degreaser to remove dirt and old lubricant, then apply fresh chain oil or wax. Avoid over-lubricating, as excess lubricant can attract dirt and debris.
