---
title: Tire Maintenance
sidebar: { order: 2 }
---

Proper tire maintenance is crucial to ensure your bicycle performs effectively and safely. Check your tires regularly for wear, cracks, or punctures to avoid unexpected flats.

Inflating your tires to the correct pressure specified by the manufacturer will provide a smoother ride and reduce the risk of damage. Be sure to inspect for embedded debris, such as glass or small stones, and remove them promptly to prevent further harm.
