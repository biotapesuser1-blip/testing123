## Bicycle Basket Maintenance

To keep your bicycle basket functional and durable:

- **Clean Regularly**: Use a damp cloth and mild detergent to wipe off dirt and prevent rust. Dry thoroughly.
- **Check for Damage**: Look for broken wires, cracks, or dents and repair them promptly.
- **Secure Fastenings**: Tighten screws and bolts to ensure the basket stays in place.
- **Protect from Weather**: Use a waterproof cover in harsh conditions to guard against material degradation.
- **Don’t Overload**: Respect the weight limits to prevent damage.

A well-maintained basket enhances your riding experience while keeping your items safe and secure.
