---
title: Bicycle Cleaning
sidebar: { order: 5 }
---

Keeping your bicycle clean not only improves its appearance but also extends its lifespan. Wash your bike regularly using mild soap and water, avoiding harsh chemicals that could damage finishes.

Pay extra attention to areas like the drivetrain and wheel hubs, where dirt accumulates most. After cleaning, dry the bicycle thoroughly to prevent rust, and periodically apply protective coatings to the frame.
